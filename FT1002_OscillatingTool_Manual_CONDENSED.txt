================================================================================
QUICK SAFETY (READ FIRST)
================================================================================

⚠ WARNING: Misuse can cause electric shock, fire, or serious injury.

• Wear eye protection and a dust mask/respirator; wear hearing protection.
• Clamp your workpiece. Hold the tool with both hands.
• Remove the battery before changing accessories.
• Check walls/floors for hidden wiring, pipes, and gas lines before cutting.
• Accessories get hot—allow to cool before touching.
• Keep charger dry. If working in damp locations, use an RCD-protected outlet/circuit.

⚠ DUST HAZARD: Dust from sanding/cutting may contain harmful chemicals (lead-based paint,
silica from masonry, arsenic/chromium from treated lumber). Work ventilated and wear a
proper respirator when needed.


================================================================================
TOOL OVERVIEW
================================================================================

Controls:
• Slide switch: I = ON / 0 = OFF
• Speed dial: 1–6 (higher = faster)
• 12-position accessory mount (30° increments)


================================================================================
GETTING STARTED
================================================================================

CHARGING
1. Insert battery into charger. Align terminals correctly (correct polarity).
2. Plug in (100–240V). Red = charging, Green = charged (about 3–5 hours).
3. Unplug and remove battery.

INSTALL BATTERY
• Slide in until it clicks. Remove by pressing release buttons.


================================================================================
INSTALL / REMOVE ACCESSORIES
================================================================================

⚠ Remove battery first. Wear gloves—accessories are sharp.

INSTALL
1. Choose accessory and angle (12 positions).
2. Align holes with pins on mount.
3. Insert center bolt and tighten with hex wrench (snug, not over-tight).
4. Tug accessory to confirm locked.

REMOVE
1. Remove battery.
2. Loosen bolt, remove accessory, store safely.


================================================================================
SPEED SELECTION (QUICK GUIDE)
================================================================================

• 1–2: plastics, delicate work, starting cuts
• 3–4: general cutting/sanding, drywall, scraping
• 5–6: metal cutting, grout removal, aggressive work

Start low and increase as needed.


================================================================================
CORE TASKS (SHORT)
================================================================================

CUTTING / PLUNGE CUTS
1. Mark line. Clamp work. Check for hidden utilities.
2. Run tool to speed, then cut with steady pressure—don’t force.
3. Keep hands clear; let blade do the work.

SANDING
1. Install pad and hook-and-loop paper (correct grit).
2. Light pressure; keep moving to avoid gouges.
3. Wear a respirator for dusty work.

SCRAPING
1. Use rigid scraper for stubborn material; flexible for delicate surfaces.
2. Medium speed; shallow angle; light pressure.


================================================================================
MAINTENANCE
================================================================================

⚠ Remove battery before cleaning.

• Wipe with dry/slightly damp cloth only.
• Keep vents and mount area clean.
• Replace worn accessories; store tool/accessories dry.


================================================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

PROBLEM: Won’t start
□ Battery charged and seated
□ Switch moves freely
□ Let tool cool and try again

PROBLEM: Excess vibration / poor performance
□ Accessory tight and seated on pins
□ Replace bent/dull accessory
□ Increase speed setting as needed
