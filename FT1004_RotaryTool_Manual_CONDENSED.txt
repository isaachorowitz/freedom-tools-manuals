================================================================================
QUICK SAFETY (READ FIRST)
================================================================================

⚠ WARNING: Misuse can cause electric shock, fire, or serious injury.

• Wear eye protection. Wear a dust mask/respirator for dusty work. Wear gloves as needed.
• Clamp your workpiece. Keep hands away from spinning accessories.
• Never use damaged accessories. Accessories must be rated for the tool’s speed.
• Let accessory reach full speed before contacting the work.
• Never press spindle lock while the tool is running.
• Cut-off wheels are for edge cutting only—never grind with the side.
• Wire brushes can throw bristles—use a face shield and protective clothing.

⚠ DUST HAZARD: Dust from sanding/cutting may contain harmful chemicals (lead-based paint,
silica from masonry, arsenic/chromium from treated lumber, wood dust). Work ventilated and
wear an appropriate respirator when required.

================================================================================
GETTING STARTED
================================================================================

CHARGING
1. Insert battery into charger. Align terminals correctly (correct polarity).
2. Plug in (100–240V). Red = charging, Green = charged (about 3–5 hours).
3. Unplug and remove battery.

INSTALL BATTERY
• Slide in until it clicks. Remove by pressing release buttons.


================================================================================
COLLETS & ACCESSORIES (FAST)
================================================================================

⚠ Remove battery before changing accessories.

CHANGE/INSTALL ACCESSORY
1. Remove battery.
2. Press spindle lock and loosen collet nut with spanner.
3. Insert accessory shaft fully into collet.
4. Tighten collet nut firmly (do not over-tighten).
5. Release spindle lock and tug accessory to confirm secure.

CUTTING WHEEL (MANDREL)
• Install mandrel in collet, place wheel, tighten screw snug (do not over-tighten).

SANDING DRUM/ROLL
• Loosen drum screw, slide sanding band on, tighten to expand and lock.


================================================================================
SPEED SELECTION (QUICK GUIDE)
================================================================================

Lower speeds:
• polishing, wire brushing, plastics, large diameter accessories

Higher speeds:
• cutting, drilling, small accessories, harder materials

Start low and increase. Too fast = heat + faster accessory wear.


================================================================================
CORE TASKS (SHORT)
================================================================================

CUTTING (ABRASIVE CUT-OFF)
1. Clamp work. Mark cut.
2. High speed. Light pressure. Edge only.
3. Do not twist or side-load wheel.

GRINDING/SHAPING
• Medium-high speed. Light pressure. Keep moving; avoid overheating.

SANDING
• Medium speed. Light pressure. Keep moving.

POLISHING
• Low-medium speed. Light pressure. Keep moving to avoid swirl/heat.

WIRE BRUSHING
• Medium speed. Light pressure. Wear face shield and protective clothing.

DRILLING (SMALL HOLES)
• Higher speed for most materials; lighter pressure; clear chips frequently.


================================================================================
MAINTENANCE
================================================================================

⚠ Remove battery before cleaning.

• Wipe with dry/slightly damp cloth only.
• Keep vents clear; remove dust buildup.
• Store tool/accessories dry and organized. Remove battery for long storage.


================================================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

PROBLEM: Won’t start
□ Battery charged and seated
□ Switch operates freely
□ Let tool cool and try again

PROBLEM: Excess vibration / wobble
□ Accessory fully inserted and tightened
□ Correct collet size for shaft
□ Replace bent/damaged accessory
